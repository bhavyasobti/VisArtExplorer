Welcome to the VISAP Explorer!

What is VISAP?

The VIS Arts Program (VISAP) is a vibrant mini-conference and exhibition space where visualization researchers, designers, and artists from all over the globe come together every year. This shared platform is dedicated to discussion and display of artworks that emerge from the intersection of their disciplines. Since its inception in 2013, VISAP has been a hub for groundbreaking ideas and collaborations. The VISAP Explorer, as the largest event affiliated with the IEEE VIS conference, primarily aims to cultivate fresh ideas, encourage discussion, and promote collaboration across various research and practice domains.

How to get started

To explore the archive, utilize the navbar on the top of the screen. Select 'Gallery' for a virtual gallery tour, where you can browse artworks by conference year and click on each piece to uncover its details. The 'Network' link takes you to an interactive graph, navigable by selecting years on the accompanying barchart. Delve into the connections between authors and artworks by interacting with the graph's nodes. To navigate back to this page, simply click on 'Home'.
